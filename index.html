First-year CS student putting in the structured reps — DSA sheet by sheet, concept by concept.

I'm a 2nd-year B.Tech Computer Science (Data Science & AI) student at ICFAI University, Jaipur. My foundation is built on C++, Data Structures & Algorithms, and Object-Oriented Programming — and I'm working to turn that foundation into the skill set a product-based company actually needs.
